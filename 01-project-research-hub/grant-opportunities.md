## üá≤üáæ NATIONAL GOVERNMENT GRANTS

### **1. Fundamental Research Grant Scheme (FRGS)**

**Funding Agency:** Ministry of Higher Education (MOHE)

**Purpose:** Support fundamental/basic research in universities

**Eligibility:**
- Malaysian public universities (primary)
- Some private universities participate
- Must be full-time academic staff
- Can be PI or co-investigator

**Funding Amount:**
- Typically: RM 50,000 - RM 150,000
- Duration: 2-3 years
- Equipment may be limited

**Research Areas Supported:**
- All disciplines including science and engineering
- Fundamental research emphasis
- Must align with national research priorities

**Application Timeline:**
- Usually annual call
- Check MOHE website for exact dates
- Preparation time: 2-3 months recommended

**Success Factors:**
- Clear fundamental research question
- Strong preliminary data
- Publication track record
- Alignment with national priorities
- Feasible budget and timeline

**Good Fit For:**
- NADES fundamental studies
- SPME kinetics and mechanism research
- Green chemistry principles research
- Method development fundamentals

**Application Requirements:**
- Research proposal (detailed)
- Budget justification
- CV and publication list
- Ethics approval (if applicable)
- Institution endorsement

---

### **2. Malaysian Research University Network (MRUN) Grant**

**Funding Agency:** MRUN Secretariat

**Purpose:** Promote collaboration between research universities

**Eligibility:**
- Faculty at MRUN member universities
- Collaborative projects (multi-university)
- At least 2 institutions involved

**MRUN Member Universities:**
- Universiti Malaya (UM)
- Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia (UKM)
- Universiti Putra Malaysia (UPM)
- Universiti Sains Malaysia (USM)
- Universiti Teknologi Malaysia (UTM)

**Funding Amount:**
- RM 100,000 - RM 300,000
- Duration: 2-3 years
- Larger projects possible

**Good Fit For:**
- Your USM collaboration (if at MRUN university)
- Multi-site analytical method validation
- Collaborative green chemistry projects
- Malaysian medicinal plants studies

**Success Factors:**
- Strong collaborative team
- Clear division of roles
- Complementary expertise
- Shared resources/facilities
- National impact

---

### **3. Transdisciplinary Research Grant Scheme (TRGS)**

**Funding Agency:** MOHE

**Purpose:** Support interdisciplinary research addressing complex problems

**Eligibility:**
- Academic staff at Malaysian universities
- Must involve multiple disciplines
- Industry collaboration encouraged

**Funding Amount:**
- RM 200,000 - RM 500,000
- Duration: 3 years
- Larger budget for complex projects

**Good Fit For:**
- Single-cell metabolomics (chemistry + biology + medicine)
- NADES drug delivery (chemistry + pharmacy)
- Green analytical methods for industry
- Medicinal plant chemistry + traditional medicine

**Success Factors:**
- True interdisciplinarity (not just multi-author)
- Complex problem requiring multiple approaches
- Clear integration plan
- Strong team with complementary skills
- Translational impact

---

### **4. Long Term Research Grant Scheme (LRGS)**

**Funding Agency:** MOHE

**Purpose:** Large-scale, long-term strategic research

**Eligibility:**
- Established researchers
- Multi-institutional
- Strategic national importance

**Funding Amount:**
- RM 2,000,000 - RM 10,000,000+
- Duration: 5-7 years
- Comprehensive funding

**Good Fit For:**
- Established research program (future, not immediate)
- Comprehensive green analytical chemistry program
- National analytical chemistry capacity building
- Large-scale medicinal plant studies

**Success Factors:**
- Established track record
- National strategic importance
- Strong consortium
- Clear deliverables
- Industry/government alignment

**Note:** Highly competitive, typically for senior established researchers. Target for year 3-5 of your program.
