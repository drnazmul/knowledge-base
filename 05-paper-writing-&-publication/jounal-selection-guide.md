# Journal Selection Guide

**Choosing the Right Journal for Your Research**

**Last Updated:** January 2025

---

## üéØ DECISION FRAMEWORK

**Choose journal based on:**
1. **Scope fit** (Does your work match the journal's focus?)
2. **Impact factor** (How prestigious/visible?)
3. **Audience** (Who reads this journal?)
4. **Timeline** (How fast to publication?)
5. **Success likelihood** (Can you get in?)
6. **Open access?** (Cost vs. reach considerations)

## üìä JOURNAL TIERS FOR ANALYTICAL CHEMISTRY

### **TIER 1: Top International Journals** (IF > 6.0)

**Analytical Chemistry (ACS)**
- Impact Factor: ~7.4
- Scope: All analytical chemistry, strong method development
- Audience: Broad analytical chemistry community
- Timeline: 3-6 months to decision
- **Your Fit:** NADES-SPME with strong novelty/performance
- Notes: Competitive but achievable with solid work
- **Submit if:** You have excellent data, novel approach, broad impact

**Nature Analytical Chemistry (discontinued, now part of Nature Protocols)**

**Green Chemistry (RSC)**
- Impact Factor: ~9.8
- Scope: Green chemistry specifically
- Audience: Green chemistry researchers, industry
- Timeline: 2-4 months
- **Your Fit:** NADES work emphasizing green aspects
- Notes: Wants environmental/sustainability impact quantified
- **Submit if:** Strong green metrics, real applications

### **TIER 2: Strong Specialized Journals** (IF 4.0-6.0)

**Talanta**
- Impact Factor: ~6.1
- Scope: Analytical chemistry, sensors, methods
- Audience: Analytical chemists, applied focus
- Timeline: 2-3 months
- **Your Fit:** NADES-SPME, most analytical work
- Notes: High volume, good acceptance rate if solid
- **Submit if:** Solid method, good validation, real samples

**Journal of Chromatography A**
- Impact Factor: ~4.1
- Scope: Separation science
- Audience: Chromatography specialists
- Timeline: 2-4 months
- **Your Fit:** SPME methods, chromatographic analysis
- Notes: Technical rigor important
- **Submit if:** Chromatography-focused work

**Analytica Chimica Acta**
- Impact Factor: ~6.2
- Scope: Analytical chemistry methods
- Audience: Analytical chemists
- Timeline: 2-3 months
- **Your Fit:** Most analytical methods
- Notes: Similar to Talanta, Elsevier

---

### **TIER 3: Good Specialized Journals** (IF 2.0-4.0)

**Journal of Molecular Liquids**
- Impact Factor: ~6.0
- Scope: Liquid properties, ionic liquids, NADES
- Audience: Physical chemistry, NADES community
- Timeline: 2-3 months
- **Your Fit:** NADES characterization, properties
- Notes: Good for NADES-focused work
- **Submit if:** Emphasizing NADES chemistry

**Microchemical Journal**
- Impact Factor: ~5.0
- Scope: Analytical methods, micro-scale
- Timeline: 2-3 months
- **Your Fit:** SPME, microextraction
- Notes: Accepts solid method papers

**Analytical and Bioanalytical Chemistry (ABC)**
- Impact Factor: ~4.3
- Scope: Analytical methods, bioanalytical
- Timeline: 3-5 months
- **Your Fit:** Bioanalytical SPME work
- Notes: Springer, good reputation

**Separation Science Plus**
- Scope: Separation methods
- **Your Fit:** SPME methods
- Notes: Newer Wiley journal

## üí∞ OPEN ACCESS CONSIDERATIONS

**Traditional (Subscription):**
- No author fee
- Limited free access
- Available through university libraries

**Open Access:**
- Author pays ($1,500-$5,000 USD)
- Freely available to all
- Higher visibility/citations
- Check if university has agreements (discounts)

**Hybrid:**
- Traditional journal with OA option
- Pay for OA in subscription journal

**Your Considerations:**
- Funding available? (Some grants cover OA)
- Career stage? (Visibility important early career)
- Audience? (Industry readers may prefer OA)

## ‚è±Ô∏è PUBLICATION TIMELINE

**Typical Timeline:**

1. **Submission:** Day 0
2. **Initial screening:** 1-2 weeks (can be desk rejected)
3. **Sent to reviewers:** Week 2-3
4. **Reviews received:** Week 8-12 (varies widely!)
5. **Revision submitted:** 2-4 weeks after decision
6. **Second review:** 2-4 weeks
7. **Acceptance:** Week 16-24 typical
8. **Online publication:** 2-4 weeks after acceptance
9. **Print publication:** Varies (months)

**Fast Journals:**
- Green Chemistry: 2-3 months total
- Talanta: 2-4 months

**Slow Journals:**
- Some can take 6-12 months
