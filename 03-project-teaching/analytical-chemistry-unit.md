## üß™ LABORATORY COMPONENT

### **Lab 1: SPME Method Development (6 hours)**

**Objective:** Develop and optimize SPME method for target analyte

**Week 1 (3 hours): Fiber Selection**
- Test 3 different fiber coatings
- Extract from standard solutions
- Analyze by GC or HPLC
- Compare extraction efficiency
- Select best fiber

**Week 2 (3 hours): Optimization**
- Optimize extraction time
- Optimize temperature
- Test matrix effects
- Validate selected conditions
- Calculate figures of merit (LOD, precision)

**Assessment:** Lab report with method development rationale

---

### **Lab 2: LC-MS/MS Quantitation (6 hours)**

**Objective:** Develop quantitative method for pharmaceutical in plasma

**Week 1 (3 hours): Method Setup**
- Standard curve preparation
- Internal standard selection
- Chromatography optimization
- MS/MS transition selection

**Week 2 (3 hours): Validation**
- Accuracy (spiked samples)
- Precision (replicates)
- Matrix effects assessment
- Calculate validation parameters

**Assessment:** Validation report following ICH Q2

---

### **Lab 3: Green Analytical Methods Comparison (4 hours)**

**Objective:** Compare traditional vs. green extraction methods

**Activity:**
- Traditional: Liquid-liquid extraction with organic solvents
- Green: SPME or NADES-based extraction
- Same target analyte and matrix
- Quantitative comparison:
  - Extraction efficiency
  - Time required
  - Solvent consumption
  - Cost analysis
  - GAPI score calculation
  - Safety comparison

**Assessment:** Comparative analysis report with recommendation

### **2. Troubleshooting Workshops**

**Based on Real Problems:**
Present scenario (e.g., "Your SPME reproducibility is terrible, RSD > 20%")

Students work in groups to:
1. List possible causes
2. Design diagnostic experiments
3. Propose solutions
4. Justify recommendations

Based on actual problems I've solved in my career.

### **3. Method Development Competition**

**Semester Project:**
- Teams compete to develop best method for assigned application

Motivates students, develops competitive skills, fun!
