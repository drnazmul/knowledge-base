How ChatGPT saved me 2 hours on method development

Real example from my work at Deciem.

I needed to develop an LC-MS method for 5 cosmetic active ingredients in aqueous formulation.

Traditional approach:
- Literature review: 2-3 hours
- Trial and error: days
- Optimization: more days

What I did instead:

My ChatGPT Prompt: "I need to analyze retinol, niacinamide, ascorbic acid, hyaluronic acid, and peptides in water-based cosmetic formula using LC-MS/MS. Suggest: 1) Sample prep 2) Column 3) Mobile phase 4) Internal standards 5) Potential interferences"

Got in 2 minutes:

- Protein precipitation with acetonitrile
- C18 column, 2.1x50mm, 5Î¼m
- Methanol/water with 0.1% formic acid gradient
- Deuterated analogs as internal standards
- Matrix effects warning (excipients, preservatives)

Perfect? No. 
Time saved? YES. 

Still needed my 14 years of expertise? Absolutely.

My new workflow:
- ChatGPT direction (15 min)
- Verify with literature (30 min vs 2 hrs)
- Test approach
- Optimize with my brain
- Time saved: 40-50% on planning

AI does not replace your chemistry knowledge, at least now. It accelerates the boring parts so you spend more time on what matters.
