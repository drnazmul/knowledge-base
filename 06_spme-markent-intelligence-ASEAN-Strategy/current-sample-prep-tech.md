âœ… **Flexible** - pH, solvent, ratio adjustments  
âœ… **Inexpensive equipment** - $500-5,000 total setup

âŒ **High solvent consumption** - 10-50 mL per sample

âŒ **Emulsion formation** - common problem, hard to troubleshoot
