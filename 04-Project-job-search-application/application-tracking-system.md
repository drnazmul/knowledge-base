## ðŸ“ž FOLLOW-UP GUIDELINES

### **When to Follow Up:**

**1 Week After Deadline:**
- If no acknowledgment received
- Brief email: "Confirming receipt of application for [position]"

**After Timeline They Gave:**
- If they said "Decisions by March 1" and it's March 15
- Polite inquiry about status

**After Interview:**
- Thank-you email within 24 hours (ALWAYS)
- Follow-up 2 weeks later if no communication

### **When NOT to Follow Up:**
- Before deadline
- Within timeline they provided
- More than once without response
- After rejection (unless seeking feedback)

## ðŸŒŸ MOTIVATION & MINDSET

**Remember:**
- Job search is a numbers game (rejection is normal!)
- Quality > quantity (better 3 strong applications than 10 mediocre)
- You only need ONE yes (the right job)
- Your unique background (research + industry) is valuable
- Persistence pays off

**When Discouraged:**
- Review your accomplishments (17 papers, 1400 citations, $50K savings)
- Talk to mentor or supportive friend
- Take break from applications (avoid burnout)
- Focus on what you can control (application quality, not decision timeline)

**Celebrate Wins:**
- Application submitted (small win!)
- Interview invitation (big win!)
- Positive interaction (encouraging sign!)
- Learning from rejection (growth!)
