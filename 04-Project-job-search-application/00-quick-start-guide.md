## üö´ COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

**1. Generic Applications**
- Don't use same materials for all universities
- Always customize for specific institution
- Reference their faculty, programs, initiatives

**2. Rushing**
- Start applications 1-2 weeks before deadline minimum
- Allow time for review and refinement
- Don't submit at last minute

**3. Ignoring Job Posting**
- Address every requirement mentioned
- Use their language/keywords
- Show you've read posting carefully

**4. Underselling Yourself**
- Don't be modest to point of weakness
- Provide evidence and quantification
- Your industry experience is VALUABLE - emphasize it!

**5. Forgetting Follow-Up**
- Thank-you emails within 24 hours
- Track all applications
- Follow up appropriately
